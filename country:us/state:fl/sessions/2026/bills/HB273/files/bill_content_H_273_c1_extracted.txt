1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to special districts; transferring,
3 renumbering, and amending s. 189.056, F.S.; defining
4 the term "downtown development district"; providing
5 that the boundaries of a downtown development district
6 may only be changed in a specified manner; requiring
7 the adopted budget of a downtown development district
8 to contain specified information; prohibiting certain
9 expenditures from exceeding a specified percentage of
10 such budget; defining the term "administrative and
11 overhead expenditures"; requiring such budget to be
12 approved in a specified manner; amending s. 215.971,
13 F.S.; revising agency agreements that provide state
14 financial assistance to recipients or subrecipients to
15 include specified special districts as an entity to
16 which such agency may provide for the payment of
17 invoices under specified circumstances; providing
18 construction; amending s. 288.0656, F.S.; revising the
19 definition of "rural community" to include specified
20 special districts; providing an effective date.
