CS/HB 31 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to recognizing Judea and Samaria;
3 providing a short title; creating s. 1.016, F.S.;
4 providing legislative intent; defining the terms
5 "official government material" and "state agency";
6 prohibiting the use of the term "West Bank" in
7 official government materials; prohibiting the use of
8 moneys to create official government materials using
9 such term; requiring instructional materials and
10 library media center collections adopted or acquired
11 by certain entities on or after a specified date to
12 refer to a certain area as Judea and Samaria;
13 prohibiting instructional materials and library media
14 center collections adopted or acquired by certain
15 entities on or after a specified date from using the
16 term "West Bank"; providing an effective date.
17
18 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:

21 and Samaria Act."
