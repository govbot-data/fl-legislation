1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to veterinary prescription disclosure;
3 creating s. 474.224, F.S.; requiring a veterinarian to
4 inform a client of the right to receive a written
5 prescription or the option to have the prescription
6 filled at the veterinary establishment; specifies
7 certain transmission methods for "written
8 prescriptions"; requires the disclosure to include an
9 acknowledgement signed by the client; requires the
10 acknowledgement to include certain information;
11 prohibiting certain statements, notifications,
12 warnings, or assumptions; requiring posting of certain
13 information; providing requirements therefor;
14 providing exceptions; providing an effective date.
