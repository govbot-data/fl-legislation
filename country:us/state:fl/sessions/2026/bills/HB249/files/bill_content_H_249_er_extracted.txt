2 An act relating to the designation of the official
3 state flagship; amending s. 15.0465, F.S.;
4 redesignating the official state flagship as the S.S.
5 American Victory; providing an effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
8
9 Section 1. Section 15.0465, Florida Statutes, is amended
10 to read:
11 15.0465 Official state flagship.—The S.S. American
12 Victory, a 455-foot historic steamship, built in 1944 schooner
13 Western Union, a 130-foot historic sailing vessel of the tall
14 ship class, built in Key West and first launched in 1939, is
15 designated the official state flagship.
16 Section 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2026.
