1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to public records; amending s.
3 119.071, F.S.; providing an exemption from public
4 records requirements for the personal identifying and
5 location information of current emergency department
6 physicians and the spouses and certain children of
7 such emergency department physicians; providing for
8 retroactive application; requiring certain agencies
9 that are custodians of exempt information to maintain
10 the exempt status of such information under certain
11 circumstances; specifying procedures for requesting an
12 agency to maintain the exempt status of such
13 information until the exemption no longer applies to
14 the individual; providing for future legislative
15 review and repeal of the exemption; providing a
16 statement of public necessity; providing an effective
17 date.

78 have occurred. Such emergency department physicians are

80 intimidation perpetrated by patients or relatives of patients

89 that the harm that may result from the release of such personal
90 identifying and location information outweighs any public
91 benefit that may be derived from the disclosure of the
