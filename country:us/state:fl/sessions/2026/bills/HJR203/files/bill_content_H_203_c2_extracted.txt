179 ARTICLE VIII
180 LOCAL GOVERNMENT
181 SECTION 7. Prohibition of reductions in local first
182 responder funding.—Beginning with the 2027-2028 local fiscal
183 year, the total funding provided by each local government for
184 services provided by law enforcement, firefighters, and other
185 first responders, as provided by general law, may not be less
186 than such jurisdiction's total budgeted amount for such services
187 in either the 2025-2026 or 2026-2027 local fiscal year,
188 whichever was higher, notwithstanding any reduction in ad
189 valorem revenue that may result from the amendment to Article
190 VII, approved by voters on November 3, 2026.

212 REDUCTION OF HOMESTEAD PROPERTY TAXES; FIRST RESPONDER
213 FUNDING REQUIREMENT.—Beginning January 1, 2027, the amendment
214 reduces non-school homestead property taxes by annually
215 increasing the homestead exemption by $100,000 each year for ten
216 years, then making such properties fully exempt from non-school
217 property taxes beginning January 1, 2037. The amendment also
218 prohibits local governments from reducing first responder
219 funding below the amount budgeted in local fiscal year 2025-2026
220 or 2026-2027, whichever was greater.
