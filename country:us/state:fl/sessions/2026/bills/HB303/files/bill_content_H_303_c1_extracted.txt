F L O R I D A H O U S E O F R E P R E S E N T A T I V E S
CS/HB 303 2026
1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to human trafficking education for
3 initial nurse licensure; amending s. 464.008, F.S.;
4 requiring completion of a course on human trafficking
5 for the initial licensure of registered nurses or
6 licenses practical nurses, beginning on a specified
7 date; providing an effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
10
11 Section 1. Paragraph (e) is added to subsection (1) of
12 section 464.008, Florida Statutes, to read:
13 464.008 Licensure by examination.—
14 (1) Any person desiring to be licensed as a registered
15 nurse or licensed practical nurse shall apply to the department
16 to take the licensure examination. The department shall examine
17 each applicant who:
18 (e) Beginning July 1, 2027, has completed a 2-hour course
19 on human trafficking. The course must include the content
20 required of the human trafficking continuing education course
21 required under s. 464.013(3)(c).
22 Section 2. This act shall take effect July 1, 2026.
