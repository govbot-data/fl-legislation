228 ARTICLE VIII
229 LOCAL GOVERNMENT
230 SECTION 7. Prohibition of reductions in local first
231 responder funding.—Beginning with the 2027-2028 local fiscal
232 year, the total funding provided by each local government for
233 services provided by law enforcement, firefighters, and other
234 first responders, as provided by general law, may not be less
235 than such jurisdiction's total budgeted amount for such services
236 in either the 2025-2026 or 2026-2027 local fiscal year,
237 whichever was higher, notwithstanding any reduction in ad
238 valorem revenue that may result from the amendment to Article
239 VII approved by voters on November 3, 2026.
240
241 ARTICLE XII
242 SCHEDULE
243 Modification of limitations on assessment increases for
244 both homestead and nonhomestead property; prohibition of first
245 responder funding reductions.—This section, the amendment to
246 Section 4 of Article VII modifying the limitations on assessment
247 increases for both homestead and nonhomestead property, and the
248 creation of Section 7 of Article VIII prohibiting local
249 governments from reducing first responder funding below a
250 specified level shall take effect January 1, 2027.

257 ARTICLE XII
258 LIMITING PROPERTY TAX ASSESSMENT INCREASES; FIRST RESPONDER
259 FUNDING REQUIREMENT.—Effective January 1, 2027, assessment
260 increases for non-school homestead property taxes happen every
261 third year, limited to three percent or the change in the
262 Consumer Price Index, whichever is lower. Assessment increases
263 on nonhomestead property are limited to 15 percent every third
264 year. Non-school assessments cannot increase if the just value
265 has decreased over prior three years. Local governments cannot
266 reduce first responder funding below fiscal year 2025-2026 or
267 2026-2027 budgeted levels, whichever was greater.
