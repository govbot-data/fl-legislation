213 ARTICLE VIII
214 LOCAL GOVERNMENT
215 SECTION 7. Prohibition of reductions in local first
216 responder funding.—Beginning with the 2027-2028 local fiscal
217 year, the total funding provided by each local government for
218 services provided by law enforcement, firefighters, and other
219 first responders, as provided by general law, may not be less
220 than such jurisdiction's total budgeted amount for such services
221 in either the 2025-2026 or 2026-2027 local fiscal year,
222 whichever was higher, notwithstanding any reduction in ad
223 valorem revenue that may result from the amendment to Article
224 VII approved by voters on November 3, 2026.

226 ARTICLE XII
227 SCHEDULE
228 Modification of limitations on assessment increases for
229 both homestead and nonhomestead property; prohibition of first
230 responder funding reductions.—This section, the amendment to
231 Section 4 of Article VII modifying the limitations on assessment
232 increases for both homestead and nonhomestead property, and the
233 creation of Section 7 of Article VIII prohibiting local
234 governments from reducing first responder funding below a
235 specified level shall take effect January 1, 2027.
