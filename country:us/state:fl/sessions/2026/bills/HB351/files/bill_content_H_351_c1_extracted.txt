1 A bill to be entitled
2 An act relating to concurrent legislative jurisdiction
3 over United States military installations; creating s.
4 250.031, F.S.; providing that this state may accept
5 the relinquishment of exclusive legislative
6 jurisdiction from the United States over United States
7 military installations located within the boundaries
8 of this state; providing that this state has
9 concurrent legislative jurisdiction with the United
10 States over those United States military
11 installations; providing procedures and requirements
12 therefor; providing an effective date.
13
14 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
15
16 Section 1. Section 250.031, Florida Statutes, is created
17 to read:
18 250.031 United States military installations; concurrent
19 legislative jurisdiction.—
20 (1) This state may accept the relinquishment of exclusive
21 legislative jurisdiction from the United States in accordance
22 with this section. This state has concurrent legislative
23 jurisdiction with the United States over any United States
24 military installation property indicated pursuant to this
25 section for as long as the United States controls the property.
