F L O R I D A H O U S E O F R E P R E S E N T A T I V E S
ENROLLED
HB 327, Engrossed 1 2026 Legislature
1
2 An act relating to the uterine fibroid research
3 database; amending s. 381.9312, F.S.; deleting a
4 prohibition on the inclusion of personal identifying
5 information in the uterine fibroid research database;
6 providing a contingent effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Florida:
9
10 Section 1. Paragraph (c) of subsection (2) of section
11 381.9312, Florida Statutes, is amended to read:
12 381.9312 Uterine fibroid research database; education and
13 public awareness.—
14 (2) UTERINE FIBROID RESEARCH DATABASE.—
15 (c) The database may not include any personal identifying
16 information of women diagnosed with or treated for uterine
17 fibroids.
18 Section 2. This act shall take effect on the same date
19 that HB 1515 or similar legislation takes effect, if such
20 legislation is adopted in the same legislative session or an
21 extension thereof and becomes a law.
